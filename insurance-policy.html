Transit Insurance

Last updated: March 24, 2026. Protect your valuables covering all transit risks.

Why Transit Insurance is Mandatory for Inter-City Moves

Road conditions, unpredictable weather, and third-party accidents are variables beyond control. Our Transit Insurance acts as an financial shield, ensuring 100% peace of mind when relocating across states.

At MST Packers & Movers, we execute every relocation with military precision and the highest quality packing materials. However, transit inherently involves minimal risks. This Insurance Policy framework details how coverage works, how to declare value, and the seamless process of claiming damages in the rare event of an incident.

1. Types of Coverage Offered

We provide comprehensive All-Risk Transit Insurance exclusively underwritten by premier National Insurance providers.

  • All-Risk Transit Insurance (Recommended): Covers damages caused during transit including accidents, fire, theft (of the entire truck), and natural calamities. Premium is calculated at 3% of the Total Declared Value.
  • Basic Coverage: If a client opts out of comprehensive insurance (requires a signed waiver), liability is restricted aggressively to local transport carrier laws (typically ₹100 per 10kg), solely applicable to total truck loss.

2. The Declaration of Value Process

To acquire insurance, the client is required to provide a 'Declared Value' for their goods before the move commences.

  • An inventory list will be generated by our surveyors.
  • The client must declare the current depreciated market value against the high-value items (e.g., TVs, refrigerators, furniture, vehicles).
  • Note on Under-Insurance: If goods are insured for less than their actual value, any claim settlement will be processed proportionately. (e.g., If a ₹1,00,000 TV is insured for ₹50,000, and suffers 50% damage requiring ₹25,000 repair, the payout will be proportionate to the short margin).

3. What is Excluded (Not Covered)

While our policy is comprehensive, standard insurance exclusions apply globally to relocation logistics. Exclusions include, but are not limited to:

  • Internal Mechanical Derangement: Electrical or mechanical failures of appliances (e.g., a fridge compressor failing to start or a PC motherboard malfunctioning) where no external physical damage to the casing is evident.
  • Pre-Existing Damages: Scratches, dents, or wear-and-tear correctly noted on the inventory sheet prior to packing.
  • Prohibited Items: Antiques, cash, jewelry, bonds, deeds, stamps, biological materials, and perishable goods unless explicitly declared, accepted, and premium paid for specialized art logistics.
  • Items Packed by Owner (PBO): Any box packed by the customer without our physical inspection before sealing.
  • Damage due to atmospheric conditions (mold, mildew, rust) resulting from climatic changes during long inter-state travel.

4. Claim Filing Procedure

In the unfortunate event of damage, we have established an expedited claim resolution protocol:

  1. Immediate Notification: Any visible external damage to cartons or items must be noted immediately on the final Delivery Receipt/Lorry Receipt (LR) before signing it off and the delivery team leaves the premises.
  2. Photographic Evidence: Take clear, high-resolution photographs and videos of the damaged item showing the exact nature of the damage.
  3. Claim Submission: Submit a formal claim via email to claims@mstpackersmovers.com within 48 hours of delivery, attaching the LR copy, inventory sheet, and visual evidence.
  4. Survey and Assessment: For claims exceeding ₹15,000, an independent insurance surveyor will be deployed to inspect the damaged goods within 3-5 working days. The goods must not be repaired or discarded prior to this inspection.

5. Settlement Timelines

MST Packers & Movers facilitates the claim with the insurance provider vigorously on your behalf.

  • Minor Claims (Under ₹15,000): Authorized and settled directly by our internal resolution team within 7-10 working days.
  • Major Claims (Above ₹15,000): Processed by the official insurance underwriter, typically settled within 30-45 working days post-survey and document submission.
  • Deductible: All claims are subject to standard policy deductibles and depreciation as assessed by the independent surveyor.

6. Premium Payment

The insurance premium is separate from the packing and moving charges and must be paid in full before the dispatch of the vehicle from the origin city. Insurance coverage will not commence until the premium is received and the physical policy certificate is issued.
