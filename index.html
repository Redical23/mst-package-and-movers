Frequently Asked Questions

How much time does packing take?
For a standard 2BHK, it usually takes our team of 4 professionals about 4-6 hours to dismantle, pack, and properly inventory all items.
Do you provide transit insurance?
Yes! We provide comprehensive transit insurance for all your belongings. In the rare scenario of damage, you are fully covered based on declared value.
Are there any hidden costs?
Absolutely not. We pride ourselves on 100% transparent pricing. The quote provided after our pre-move survey is final, inclusive of all taxes and tolls.
Do you unpack belongings at the destination?
Yes, our door-to-door service includes unpacking heavy furniture and placing them as per your instruction, along with removing debris.
